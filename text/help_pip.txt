	All commands can be entered as any case: move north / Move North / MOVE NORTH

			       	       ===(Pip-Boy)===
  
  View All Items)-           Open your Inventory and view Items by typing the 'i' command.
 		Example: :>i  {ENTER KEY}

                                  ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

  Quest Menu)-               Open Quest menu to view objectives by typing the 'q' command.
		Example: :>q  {ENTER KEY}

                                  ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

  Close Pip-Boy)-            Close the Pip-Boy menu by typing the 'c' command.
		Example: :>c  {ENTER KEY}
